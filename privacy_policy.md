## 1. Information We Collect

To provide a secure investment portal and decentralized production network, we collect several types of information:

### A. Personal Information You Provide
*   **Account Registration Details:** When you create an account, we collect your **Name, Email Address, Contact Number (WhatsApp/Phone),** and account passwords.
*   **Verification & KYC Information:** To comply with financial regulations and establish producer credits, we may collect verification documents, government IDs, address proofs, and investor signatures.
*   **Financial & Investment Data:** We track investment amounts, transaction receipts, bank transfer confirmation slips, and payment details you upload to facilitate and verify investments.

### B. Device and Usage Data
*   **Device Identifiers:** We may collect device-specific information (such as your mobile device ID, IP address, operating system version, and browser type) to optimize performance and prevent unauthorized account access.
*   **Usage Log Data:** We log system events, page visits, and actions taken within the App to monitor stability and security.

### C. Permissions
*   **Internet Access:** The App requires network permissions to sync with our database services (Supabase/Firebase) and process investments in real time.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following specific and transparent purposes:
1.  **To Provide and Maintain Our Service:** Including managing user registration, showing active projects, and keeping track of producer tiers.
2.  **To Facilitate Investments:** Verifying payment confirmations, processing bank transfer claims, and awarding appropriate Producer Tiers.
3.  **To Verify Identity (KYC):** Ensuring transactions are legitimate and preventing financial fraud on the platform.
4.  **To Communicate with You:** Sending notifications, WhatsApp support messages, and transaction updates.
5.  **To Ensure App Security:** Monitoring for suspicious activity, protecting user accounts, and enforcing our terms.
