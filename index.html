About Bharat Film Industry (BFI)

Bharat Film Industry (BFI) is pioneering the future of film investment and co-production in India. Operating as a decentralized production network, BFI connects independent screenwriters, creative directors, and capital partners directly, eliminating traditional studio intermediaries and gatekeepers.

By combining secure milestone-based escrow accounts with dynamic market viability evaluations, BFI ensures that every rupee deployed is spent transparently. When a film listed on our script marketplace succeeds at the box office or theatrical releases, co-producers and investors participate directly in profit-sharing.

Featured Cinematic Works: Preema Preethi

A prime showcase of the BFI ecosystem is the acclaimed short film Preema Preethi, directed by our Founder & CEO, Prathapaneni Roopchandu. Serving as a visionary director, Roopchandu has showcased the potential of independent creative freedom combined with structured production execution.

BFI Script & Screenplay Marketplace

Our platform hosts active movie projects across popular genres including Action/Thriller, Sci-Fi Noir, Historical Epic, and Cinematic Drama. Investors can deploy capital securely to finance movie nodes, while writers and crew can pitch creative portfolios directly to verified producers.

  • Writers: Register and protect your screenplay on the official BFI network.
  • Directors: Broadcast your bidding node and synopsis to raise capital.
  • Investors: Back high-potential scripts with transparent escrows and clear return models.
